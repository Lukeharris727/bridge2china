Requirements

  • Must be a national of the US, UK, Ireland, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa or one of the following North-West European countries: Belgium, Netherlands, Sweden, Norway, Finland, Denmark, Germany, Austria
  • Have an excellent command of both spoken and written English
  • Hold a Bachelor degree or above
  • Be available to work in China for 1 full academic year
  • Have a genuine respect for and interest in discovering Chinese culture
  • No Criminal record

Desirable

  • Preference will be given to native speakers from the following countries: US, UK, Ireland, Canada, Australia, New Zealand and South Africa
  • Degree (Bachelor or Master) in Education or related fields
  • TEFL/TESOL certificate (required to teach English)
  • Prior teaching experience or demonstrable experience working with children
  • Beginner level Mandarin Chinese
  • Many reasons for wanting to move to China
